## Introduction to Peter Chen

**Peter Chen** introduced the Entity-Relationship model in 1976, which became the foundation for database design. His notation is one of the most widely used methods for creating ER diagrams.

### Why Peter Chen Notation?

- âœ… Intuitive and easy to understand
- âœ… Industry standard
- âœ… Clearly shows entities, attributes, and relationships
- âœ… Supports both detailed and simplified views

| Notation | Minimum | Maximum | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
